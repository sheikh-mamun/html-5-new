The COVID-19 pandemic, also known as the coronavirus pandemic

,

severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2).

The novel virus was first identified in an outbreak in the Chinese city of Wuhan in December 2019. Attempts to contain it there failed, allowing the virus to spread to other areas of Asia and then worldwide in early 2020.
